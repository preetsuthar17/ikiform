## 1. Scope
This page summarizes how Ikiform supports GDPR obligations for customers and data subjects.

## 2. Roles
Customers are typically data controllers for submitted form data. Ikiform generally acts as a data processor.

## 3. Data Processing
Processing includes storing form schemas, collecting responses, and enabling reporting and integrations configured by customers.

## 4. Security Measures
Ikiform applies access control, encryption in transit, monitoring, and incident response practices.

## 5. Data Subject Requests
We provide product and support mechanisms that help customers respond to access, deletion, and correction requests.

## 6. International Transfers
Where transfers occur, we rely on contractual and organizational safeguards appropriate to applicable law.
