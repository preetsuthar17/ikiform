We use data to provide the service, secure accounts, process billing, support users, and improve platform quality.

## 4. Legal Bases
Where applicable, we rely on contract performance, legitimate interests, consent, and legal obligations.

## 5. Retention
We retain data for as long as necessary for service delivery, legal compliance, and dispute resolution.

## 7. User Rights
Depending on jurisdiction, users may request access, correction, deletion, portability, or restriction of their personal data.
