## 1. Purpose
This DPA describes processing terms between Ikiform and customers using Ikiform to process personal data.

## 2. Subject Matter
The processing covers form configuration, response collection, storage, export, and customer-requested integrations.

## 3. Duration
Processing continues for the duration of the applicable customer relationship unless otherwise required by law.

## 4. Processor Obligations
Ikiform implements safeguards, confidentiality controls, and subprocessors management consistent with applicable data protection law.

## 5. Customer Responsibilities
Customers are responsible for lawful instructions, notices to respondents, and valid legal basis for processing.

## 6. Deletion and Return
Upon contract end and as requested, customer data is returned or deleted, subject to legal retention obligations.

## 7. Contact
For DPA requests, contact [hi@ikiform.com](mailto:hi@ikiform.com).
